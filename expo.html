HOW TO PLAY

Reroll (🔃) for new enemies. 25% chance for a Gift (🎁), 25% for a stronger Elite.

XP Bar: Fills when defeating enemies. When full, pick an Upgrade.

Fortunes (🔮): Make a permanent Sacrifice to gain a powerful, run-altering Fortune.

Magic Bar: Fuels powerful spells.

Moon Arts (🌒): Spend Moonstones on limited-use skills in the ? slots.

Luck (🍀): Choose luck pre-battle. Activate in combat for a chance at a powerful bonus.

Synergies: Combine equipment, Fortunes, and abilities for unique builds.

Unlocks: Surviving to later waves can unlock permanent rewards, even in death.
